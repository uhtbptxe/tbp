Important Dates for Spring 2014 Candidates

Bent Polish:
The Bent Polish will take place on Friday, April 11th between 10AM and 2PM . You are required to polish the bent for at least 1 hour. Please sign up using the link below. You will meet us at the Bent in front of the old Engineering Building at the time you signed up. Please wear something comfortable that you do not mind getting dirty. Also, if you have yet to turn the Signature Tau Beta Block or the Volunteer Activities List please bring those to the Bent Polish to turn in.

> Bent Polish Sign-up Sheet

Initiation:
Initiation will be held on Sunday, April 13th. Please arrive at the Engineering Commons (The Pit) by 3:45PM. Remember that Initiation is REQUIRED in order to become a member of Tau Beta Pi. Please wear business professional clothing! We will be taking pictures in front of the newly polished Bent after the initiation so you should look your best!

Banquet:
The banquet will also be held on Sunday,April 13th at 6PM after the initiation. Please RSVP with the link below. Also, if you have any guests but have not paid for them ($20 each guest) please do so at the Bent Polish on Friday or bring $20 in cash on Sunday. Feel free to invite family, friends or any non-TBP member you want. Location: TBA
