Undergraduate Eligibility Requirements (Spring 2015)

We seek engineering students who have academically excelled in the program to be a part of our organization. In order to receive an invitation to join TBP, a student must rank (based on cumulative GPA) in the top 1/8 of their junior class or the top 1/5 of their senior class. In addition, a student must have passed the required freshman and sophomore courses with at least a C- or better. Students who have not completed or are currently enrolled in these courses are not eligible for membership. The required courses based on major are listed under Undergraduate Course Requirements.
