Application Process

 

Step 1:

Ensure  you have fulfilled the Undergraduate Course Requirements

 

>> View the Undergraduate Course Requirements

 

Step 2:

Fill out an Application Form

Complete the form by February 26, 2015.

 

>> Application Form

 

Step 3:

 

 

 

 

Email your Unofficial Transcript and Resume

Complete this step by February 26, 2015.

 

    • Highlight the courses you have taken from the Undergraduate Course Requirements on your transcript.
    • Name your Transcript File: “LastName.FirstName.Transcript.pdf”
    • Name your Resume File: “LastName.FirstName.Resume.pdf”
    • Title the email subject: FirstName LastName Application
    • Email all items to txepsilon@gmail.com

 

 

For graduate students:

    • Provide a list of all degrees received and in progress (whether engineering or not) by institution, degree, major, and graduation date.
    • Provide signed letter on UH letter head with the statement "I recommend for membership in Tau Beta Pi and certify that (insert your name) has completed at least half of the required coursework, including research, for the degree (insert degree in progress here). (Due February 27)
    • Name your Transcript File: “LastName.FirstName.Transcript.pdf”
    • Name your Resume File: “LastName.FirstName.Resume.pdf”
    • Name your Recommendation File: "LastName.FirstName.Recommendation.pdf"
    • Title the email subject: FirstName LastName Application
    • Email all items to txepsilon@gmail.com

Step 4:

 

Send a Recommendation Form to TWO of your professors/faculty member

Professors must complete this form by March 26, 2015.

 

Forward the link below to your professors. One of the two professors must be a faculty member from one of the engineering, math, chemistry, or physics department. You are responsible to ensure your chosen professors complete the form in a timely manner.

 

>> Recommendation Form

 

Step 5:

Obtain signatures for your Signature Block

Complete this step by March 26, 2015.

 

TBP will provide you a Signature Block. You will obtain signatures from 6 underclassmen (freshmen and sophomores), 6 current members, 3 professors, and 3 officers. When obtaining signatures from underclassmen, you are to explain the TBP's mission. In addition, you must correctly answer a question about TBP in order to receive a signature from an officer. Questions are derived from the About Us tab.

 

>> Instructions to set up your Signature Block

 

Step 6:

Attend an EF session and two volunteering events

Complete the step by April 17, 2015.

 

Initiates are required to participate in at least one Engineering Future Session and two Volunteering Events. Sessions and events have to be completed before the Bent Polish. Please document your attendance with the provided form below. View the calendar for details.   

 

>> Print the Event Attendance Form

 

Step 7:

 

 

Pay your Membership Fee

Complete this step by March 26, 2015.

 

Lifetime membership Fee are $100 which includes the national dues, a subscription to The Bent magazine, TBP pendant with your name engraved, personalized certificate, initiation banquet, socials, and a TBP TX-Epsilon t-shirt. Tau Beta Pi accepts cash or checks. Please address checks to "Tau Beta Pi." Payment may be submitted through Paypal. However, there is a processing fee assesed by Paypal of $3.20.


 

Step 8:

 

 

Polish the UH Bent

Attend the Bent Polish on April 17, 2015.

 

You are required to polish the bent for at least one hour. We suggest that you dress appropriately as your clothing may become soiled. In the event of severe weather problems, this date will be rescheduled and you will be notified via email. If you are unable to polish on this date, please contact an officer immediately to establish an alternative time.

 

You will meet us at the Bent in front of the Engineering Building during your scheduled time slot. Please wear something comfortable that you do not mind getting ruined. You will submit your Signature Block and the Event Attendance Form to the Bent Polish.

 

>> Bent Polish Sign Up Sheet

 

Step 9:

 

Obtain and Polish Personal Bents

Complete this step by April 19, 2015.

 

TBP will provide you a personal bent at the Bent Polish Event. You are required to polish the bent on your own time prior to the banquet. The polishing of your personal bent should be completed before initiation and brought to the initiation ceremony.

 

Step 10:

Attend the Initiation Ceremony

Complete the form.

Attend the Initiation Ceremony on April 19, 2015.

 

 

Initiation is REQUIRED in order to become a member of Tau Beta Pi. This event is mandatory and alternative arrangements cannot be made for any reason. Please wear business professional clothing! Complete the form below prior to the Initiation Ceremony.

 

>> Name Pronunciation Form

 

Step 11:

 

Attend Banquet

Complete the form.

Attend the Banquet on April 19, 2015.

 

Banquet is optional. The Banquet will follow the Initiation Ceremony. However, you are strongly encouraged into go as you will receive your TBP certificate, TBP pendant with your name engraved and your T-shirt.

 

>> Banquet RSVP Form
